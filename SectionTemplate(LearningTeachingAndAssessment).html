Learning Teaching and Assessment

Our Vision

Learning, teaching and assessment are at the core of our business as an education service. Children and young people deserve the very best learning experiences all the time. These aspects are central and inextricably linked to all other areas of our work in both schools and nurseries. All staff should take account of how children and young people learn and make progress.

High quality teaching and positive relationships between staff and pupils are the basis for high quality learning experiences for all. Staff in all establishments should continue to focus on improving learning experiences and developing their pedagogy all in an effort to help pupils secure better outcomes.

Children and young people learn best when they are fully engaged in their learning and when staff speak to them regularly and clearly about their learning and progress.

Children and young people should be actively involved in their learning. This should extend beyond the walls of each classroom and playroom and align with each of the four contexts of learning from Curriculum for Excellence. The outdoor learning space can be a natural extension of the indoor environment and provide a wide range of opportunities for formal and informal learning across all curriculum and subject areas.
