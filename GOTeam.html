GO moderators

GO moderators contribute to delivering key information and resources to support staff from across the service in their daily duties. Each service area has a designated point of contact . This point of contact will be able to assist you with any specific, service-related enquiries or pass your enquiry to the relevant team.

The GO team are always happy to hear from you! If you have any questions, ideas, feedback or if you are interested in getting involved and joining the team, email us.
