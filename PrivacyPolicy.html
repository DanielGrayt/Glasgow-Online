Privacy Policy

We respect your right to privacy and your choices regarding your data. We need to process some of your personal information when you’re using our tools, but we go to great lengths to keep your digital footprint small, safe, and in your control. If you have any questions about your privacy on Glasgow Online, please reach out to our Data Protection Officer.

Manage cookies

We use cookies to store relevant data to optimise your experience on Glasgow Online.

Clear cookies

You can remove all your data from our cookies.

What cookies do we use?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer by your browser when you visit a website. Cookies can recognize and remember you, for example.

Functional Cookies

We use functional cookies to create a seamless experience for you on Glasgow Online. They are required to use our Service.

Name Description Retention period
wt_trk Store the user’s origin. 7 days
wt_from Enhances user experience. 1 year
wt_to Enhances user experience. 1 year
wt_plus Enhances user experience for Pro users. 1 year
wt_wp Enhances user experience around sign-in. 1 year
wt_newlayout Design/layout changes. 1 year
wt_tandc Store user’s acceptance of our Terms and Conditions. 1 year
wt_takeover Enhances user experience around initial visits. 1 year

Analytical Cookies

We use analytical cookies to help us understand how you use Glasgow Online. They allow us to A/B test new features, ensure that everything is working as it should, and stop bots and malicious behavior like spam. These cookies may include third-party cookies which process personal information.

Name Description Retention period
_ga Google Analytics. 2 years
wt_first_visit First visit’s Timestamp. 1 year
