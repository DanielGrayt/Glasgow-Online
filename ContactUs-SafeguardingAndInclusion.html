Care Experienced Team

The Care Experienced Team (CET) supports the improvement of education, health and wellbeing outcomes for care experienced children and young people from age 0-26 years. Our team is currently made up of:

  • Depute Head Teacher
  • Senior Education Psychologist
  • Health and Social Care Partnership (HSCP) Service Manager (0.5)

The CET work in partnership with a wide variety of partners including the Volunteer Tutor’s Organisation (VTO), Outdoor Resource Centre (ORC), MCR Pathways, Intandem mentoring project, Glasgow Life, Action for Children and Skills Development Scotland (SDS) to enable support such as tutoring, mentoring, enhanced learning opportunities, outdoor learning, counselling, transition support, wellbeing and improved sustained positive destinations.

Care Experienced Team contact details
  • cet@glasgow.gov.uk

Every Child Counts

Glasgow CREATE is part of Glasgow City Council Education Services, including Glasgow’s Instrumental Music Service and Youth Music Initiative. CREATE supports Primary, Secondary, ASN and Early Years establishments to deliver a high quality expressive arts curriculum and to raise attainment and achievement across the curriculum through developing the creativity skills and thinking differently.

Glasgow Psychological Services

The Psychological Service applies psychological knowledge and skills to promote inclusion of all children and young people to maximise their potential for learning and to promote and enhance their wellbeing. We work with and through the significant people in children's lives - parents, school staff, and other professionals. This work includes input at the level of individual schools, to groups of schools clustered into Learning Communities and to local authority and council policy to innovate and support practices that promote inclusion. We also work with and for children where there are barriers to learning that may arise from a range of factors including disabilities, health needs, family circumstances, the learning environment and social and emotional factors.
