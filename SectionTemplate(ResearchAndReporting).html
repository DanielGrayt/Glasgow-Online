Performance, Planning & Research

There are two sections within the PPR team. Planning, Performance and Research and SEEMIS Support and development.

The PPR team provides support to educational establishments and headquarters staff around all aspects of performance and planning information. This includes the collection, submission and dissemination of information for the purposes of statutory returns to the Scottish Government and also the extraction and analysis of data from Education Services MIS systems for the purposes of performance monitoring and reporting. We also contribute to the development and monitoring of Education Service Plans and the Council Strategic Plan.

We also are involved in all aspects of research from designing tools, carrying out fieldwork, analysing research findings, preparing reports and disseminating findings. As well as providing advice to others on research methodology both internally and externally.

There are several areas of work we are involved in:

Digital Inclusion

The team also provide support to schools around Digital Inclusion. On 3rd July 2020, the Scottish Government announced that they were offering a one-year grant to local authorities to help tackle the digital exclusion of children and young people across Scotland. A range of equipment has been purchased and disseminated to children and young people across the city via schools.

On 13th January 2021 the Scottish Government announced a new funding package to help local authorities to provide support to schools and families as they deal with the challenges of remote learning during lockdown.

Schools with any enquiries relating to the Digital Inclusion project (Ipads/Chromebooks/Mifi Devices) should email Digital Inclusion

SEEMIS & GLASGOW MIS

Providing support and guidance to establishments and headquarters staff to access and utilize the SEEMiS system.

The team take a lead role in the preparations for SEEMiS Next Generation which will replace the current SEEMIS system over the next few years. This includes SEEMiS Early Years and SEEMiS Schools.

Related to this project is the development of several new MIS applications including Early Years Pastoral Notes and Early Years Wellbeing as well as the ongoing developments of the FOCUS application.
