    Early Learning and Childcare

    Our Education Services has 110 Early Years Nurseries and there are also 345 Private and Voluntary childcare providers throughout the City (to include private nurseries, playgroups, child minders', Out of School Care and Crèche facilities.

    We are aware of the Importance of early years learning and have all the information you need to make the right choice for your child. We offer a wide range of information on all childcare services (0-5 years) and support services for families and providers.

    Performance, Planning & Research

    The PPR team provides support to educational establishments and headquarters staff around all aspects of performance and planning information. This includes the collection, submission and dissemination of information for the purposes of statutory returns to the Scottish Government and also the extraction and analysis of data from Education Services MIS systems for the purposes of performance monitoring and reporting. We also contribute to the development and monitoring of Education Service Plans and the Council Strategic Plan
