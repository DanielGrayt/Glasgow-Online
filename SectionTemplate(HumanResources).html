Human Resources

This Area has been developed to provide managers and staff in education establishments and headquarters with access to key HR information for Education Services.

From this Area you can access guidance documents, standard templates and stationery together with updates on developments affecting Education Services’ HR.

We aim to ensure that all documents and guidance is relevant. If you are aware of any errors in the guidance arising from your own experiences, please Contact Human Resources
