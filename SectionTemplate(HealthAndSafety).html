Health and Safety

The Council's Education Health and Safety Team will support and liaise with the Education Service Directorate and will advise all levels of management and staff in matter relating to all Health and Safety

The team will assist and advise Education Services in preparation, regular updating and implementation of the Health and Safety Policy and associated arrangements i.e Management Standards, Codes of Practice and Risk Assessment.
