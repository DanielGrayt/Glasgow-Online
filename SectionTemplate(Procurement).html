Procurement

The Education Procurement Team works in partnership with Glasgow City Council's Corporate Procurement Unit (CPU) to:

  • Advise on PECOS related matters
  • Secure compliance against corporate and collaborative contracts
  • Identify efficiency savings under new opportunities for contractual or framework agreements
  • Assist in finding a resolution for blocked invoices generated through PECOS
  • Provide authorisation on all Non PO Direct Input Invoices
  • Provide authorisation for One Time Vendor Requests

If you have an enquiry/complaint please complete the enquiry form.
