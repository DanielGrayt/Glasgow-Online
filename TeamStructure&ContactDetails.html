Health and Safety

The Council's Health and Safety Team will support and liaise with the Education Services Directorate and will advice all levels of management and staff in matter relating to all Health and Safety

The Team will assist and advice Education Services in preperation, regular updating and implementation of the Health and Safety Policy and associated arrangement i.e Management Standards, Codes of PRactice and Risk Assessments

Incident Reporting

A health and safety incident report must be submitted using the councils HANDS Incident Reporting System for all work related accident / incidents, reportable injuries, dangerous occurences, near misses, fires, gas incidents, incidents involving verbal / physical abuse and fatalities.
