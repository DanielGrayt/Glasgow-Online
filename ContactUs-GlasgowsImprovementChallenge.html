CREATE

Glasgow CREATE is part of Glasgow City Council Education Services, including Glasgow’s Instrumental Music Service and Youth Music Initiative. CREATE supports Primary, Secondary, ASN and Early Years establishments to deliver a high quality expressive arts curriculum and to raise attainment and achievement across the curriculum through developing the creativity skills and thinking differently.

PEPASS

PEPASS aims to:-

  • Support teaching and learning in Physical Education and promote citywide staff development opportunities
  • Develop an understanding of the importance of physical literacy in raising attainment
  • Lead on the development and support for National Qualifications and Broad General Education (BGE)
  • Ensure there are more and higher quality opportunities to participate in sport within schools, curricular and Out of Schools Hours
  • Motivate and inspire children and young people to participate and lead in sport and physical activity
  • Build capacity through the recruitment, retention and development of a network of volunteers to deliver sport, developing the young workforce
  • Continue to work with partners; Sportscotland, GlasgowLife, Scottish Disability Sport, Governing bodies in Sport and Play agencies to deliver high quality experiences and support network

STEM

STEM Glasgow are part of Education Services at Glasgow City Council. The STEM Glasgow consists of two STEM Development Officers who provide support for practitioners from across the city in raising awareness and attainment in STEM. This is done through:

  • Learner Experience (incl. Glasgow Challenges Climate, 3D Printing, STEMFest)
  • Staff Development (incl. Primary STEM Leaders, STEM in a Context, Secondary STEM Network & TeachMeets)
  • Partnerships (incl. Glasgow Science Centre, Education Scotland and Glasgow Life)
