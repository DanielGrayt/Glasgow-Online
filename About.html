About Glasgow Online

It’s been more than 10 years since Glasgow Online (GO) was launched by education services to improve communications and make it easier to share online resources across all of Glasgow’s schools and nurseries…and it’s time for a refresh!

The intranet site – a click that links up anyone who works in a school, nursery and headquarters – has helped to cascade news, information and good practice and been a one-stop shop for anything ‘education’ in our city.

"I'm delighted with the refresh of GO - and that in just seven months from the launch, we are recording more than 7K hits each day to the webpages.”

Douglas Hutchison
Executive Director of Education

Vision

Easy to use, up-to-date communication and resource rich webpages for Glasgow’s Education Services – everything you need to know in one place.

Aims

  • All the information and resources at the touch of a button to support Glasgow’s education sector
  • Connecting more than 300 establishments and over 10,000 staff
  • Concise, current and topical pages kept updated by subject experts
  • Effective and efficient way to share good practice and resources quickly and sustainably

Key Messages

Douglas Hutchison, the executive director of education said: “GO has become synonymous with education news and current information – if you want to know what is new and what is happening in our schools, nurseries and head office – then visit GO to find out the facts.

“Each page is kept updated by a subject expert and with a new GO tile of everyone’s iPad that information is just one click away.

“We know from analytics that one of the most frequently visited pages on the new site is the education directory, so we’ve spent time making this even more useful with additional information and links to school information.

“However, we still need everyone’s help to make GO the best it can be and why it is so important that the communication is a two-way process.

“We need to hear from schools and nurseries and our staff – news of innovative practices in our schools, celebrating achievement and human-interest stories to bring our huge education family digitally closer!”
